The Life I’ve Led

Focus: Present Perfect Tense (Experience)

I have traveled across many lands,
I have walked on golden sands.
I have seen the mountains high,
And watched the birds across the sky.
I have known the cold of winter snow,
And felt the summer’s gentle glow.
I have met people, kind and true,
And shared a laugh with quite a few.
I have learned to speak a second tongue,
I have heard the songs that others sung.
I have lived, and I have grown,
Through all the places I have known.

Grammar Check

Present Perfect: Have / Has + Past Participle

We use this to talk about life experiences when the specific time is not important.
