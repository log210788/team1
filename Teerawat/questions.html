💬 Conversation Questions

Deep Talk for Adults  ·  Teerawat's Class

😔 Regrets
  • 1. What is your biggest regret in life so far?
  • 2. Is there something you almost did — but didn't — that still crosses your mind?
  • 3. What is something you said to someone that you wish you could take back?
  • 4. Is there a person in your past you wish you had treated differently?
🌟 Dreams
  • 1. What are your biggest dreams for the future?
  • 2. If fear wasn't a factor, what would you go after in life?
  • 3. What does your ideal life look like in 10 years?
  • 4. Is there a dream you've quietly given up on? What happened?
  • 5. What is one thing you want to achieve before you die?
🏆 Role Models
  • 1. Who do you look up to most, and why?
  • 2. Who has had the biggest impact on who you are today?
  • 3. Is there someone you used to admire who let you down? What happened?
  • 4. What quality in someone else do you most wish you had?
⚡ Fear & Courage
  • 1. What is your greatest fear — be honest.
  • 2. What is the bravest thing you have ever done?
  • 3. What are you afraid people will find out about you?
  • 4. What is something that still scares you even as an adult?
❤️ People & Love
  • 1. Who is the most important person in your life right now, and why?
  • 2. What does love mean to you — in your own words?
  • 3. Is there someone you've lost touch with who you miss?
  • 4. What is the kindest thing anyone has ever done for you?
  • 5. Have you ever felt truly understood by someone? Who was it?
🪨 Hardship
  • 1. What has been the hardest moment of your life?
  • 2. What is something you survived that you didn't think you would?
  • 3. What is something you've had to forgive — yourself or someone else?
  • 4. When life got hard, what kept you going?
🪞 Self
  • 1. What are you most proud of in your life?
  • 2. What is something about yourself you are still working to change?
  • 3. What is one thing you love about yourself that you don't say out loud?
  • 4. What do you want to be remembered for?
  • 5. If you could give your younger self one piece of advice, what would it be?
